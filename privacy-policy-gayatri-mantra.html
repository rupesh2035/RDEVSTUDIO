Privacy Policy

Gayatri Mantra
Last updated: May 24, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Gayatri Mantra app handles user information. We respect your privacy and aim to provide a simple, safe, and devotional experience.

2. Information We Collect

The Gayatri Mantra app does not require login, account creation, or personal profile details. We do not collect names, phone numbers, email addresses, contacts, photos, or personal documents.

3. App Features and Local Data

Some app settings such as language preference, theme preference, font size, reminder settings, vibration preference, and jaap counter progress may be stored locally on your device to improve your app experience.

This data stays on your device and is not uploaded to our server.

4. Notifications and Reminders

The app may request notification permission to provide devotional reminders. You can enable or disable reminders anytime from the app settings or your device settings.

5. Advertising

The Gayatri Mantra app may show limited advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device-related information to serve and measure ads, as described in Google's own privacy policies.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here: Google Advertising Policy

6. Internet Usage

The core devotional features such as mantra text, audio, and basic app usage are designed to work offline. Internet may be used for ads, Play Store links, privacy policy links, or app sharing features.

7. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

8. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for general devotional use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal user data. Since the app does not require personal account data, we do not share such data with third parties.

10. Data Security

We use simple and privacy-friendly app design. Local preferences remain on your device. However, no digital system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
